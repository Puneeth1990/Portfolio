About me

Who am I?

I'm a Software Engineer at Intel Inc., in Santa Clara, California. I have completed my Master's in Computer Science - at University of Houston based in Houston, TX. I specialize in Algorithms, Web Development and User Interfaces visually beautiful and delivering delightful user experience. Alongside this, I also possess expertise in front-end development, with focus on responsiveness, friendly code and importantly something that's attractively suited to the topic :).

I enjoy understanding how great designs work and showing the same to others through good, intuitive and user-friendly experiences. My previous experience in UI/UX Design at iGATE Global Solutions Ltd. has given me great exposure in crafting a comprehensive look and feel for products, working in the agile environment and driving requirements from concept to release. Prior to this, I also had the wonderful opportunity to be a part of NIE UI/UX Group, where I enjoyed designing Mobile and Web applications, focusing on simplicity, and user friendly experience.

I love working on web design and development and have driven UI requirements from concept to deployment. Possessing skills that are a combination of design and development, I can make things beautiful, interactive and enjoyable to use.

Thank you for stopping by :)
